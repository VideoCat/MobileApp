WatzThis?

  1. Use the top row of buttons to turn the flashlight on or off, or to make the light red.
  2. Use the 2nd row of buttons to turn on and off your automatic flashing color light dance party.
  3. Increase button increases the time between color changes by 20 msec. maximum = 10,000 msec
  4. Decrease button decreases the time between color changes by 20 msec. minimum = 10 msec
  5. Reset Speed button resets the time between color changes to 500 msec.
